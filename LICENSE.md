## Research-paper figures

The research figures under `public/papers/` are third-party works and are not covered by the statement above. Each included figure is distributed under the article’s stated Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 terms, subject to its recorded attribution and third-party-material review.

For every figure, consult:

- `data/papers.ts` for the paper, authors, figure number, source, license URL, and modification notice
- `data/rights-evidence.json` for machine-readable evidence and asset hashes
- `data/RIGHTS_AUDIT.md` for the human-reviewed inclusion record

Creative Commons license: <https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/>

The CC BY 4.0 terms apply to the eligible third-party article figures, not automatically to the Paper Picture application code, branding, or design.
