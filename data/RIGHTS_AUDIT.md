# Open Graphics Collection 01 — Rights Audit

Reviewed: 12 July 2026

## Inclusion rule

An item is playable only when its article page explicitly states CC BY 4.0, its selected figure carries no separate exclusion or third-party credit, its publication metadata and author affiliation are verified on the publication page, and its attribution record is complete in `data/papers.ts`.

The game stores the publisher-provided image without editing it. Responsive display sizing is performed by the browser.

## Excluded source types

- Default arXiv distribution licenses
- CC BY-ND publications requiring extracted or modified figures
- Papers whose selected figures carry a separate third-party credit
- Merely free-to-read or open-access papers without an explicit reuse license
- Project-page images without explicit reuse permission
