## Inclusion rule

An item is playable only when its official publication page explicitly states CC BY 4.0, its selected figure caption carries no separate exclusion or third-party credit, its publication metadata and first-author affiliation are verified on that page, and its attribution record is complete. Publisher-provided figure images are stored without editing and only resized by the browser.
